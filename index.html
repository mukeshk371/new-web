Over the years since its inception in 1976, St. R.K.S. SR. SEC. SCHOOL has sensed the passage of time with various turning points that have marked its indomitable spirit of adventure. Its focus has always been on imparting quality education. Students of this school have consistently scored high marks and have excelled in co-curricular activities. An all round development of students has always been our main concern. With rapid advancement in the technology driven world today, we continue to be innovative in our method of teaching.




Student life at The First Academy is a rich and vibrant experience.

Striving to provide a well balanced spiritual, academic and social well-being, students are encouraged to both serve and participate in and beyond our community. With plenty of room for opportunity, students can engage in over forty sports, the arts, and equally many student driven clubs.
